# Master SRS: Palladium Digital Suite

## 1. Data Schema (The "Nightbane" Model)
Characters use a "Total Reconfiguration" engine.
- **Shared:** XP, PPE.
- **State-Dependent (Nightbane):** All 8 Attributes, HP, SDC, Alignment, and Skills swap when toggling between Facade and Morphus.

## 2. Attribute Forge
- Support "Pool and Assign" logic.
- Roll N+1 keep N (e.g., 4d6 keep 3).
- Soft-prompt bonus die (+1d6) on rolls of 12 (2d6) or 16-18 (3d6).

## 3. The Psychic Gate
- Save targets: 15 (None), 12 (Minor/Major), 10 (Master).
- Major Psionic Tax: Reduce O.C.C. Related Skill slots by 50% (floor).

## 4. Ability Selection
- Unified picker for Magic, Psionics, and Talents.
- Every ability must show its Source Attribution (Race, O.C.C., or Psychic Tier).

## 5. Combat HUD (The "Destiny" HUD)
- Anchored Weapon Slots with Strike/Parry/Damage.
- APM Tracker: Visual icons for actions per melee that can be "spent" and reset.
