Biomechanical: Aquatic Table
Nightbane with a Biomechanical: Aquatic Table part often
feel a need to explore the world, especially beneath the waves.
The character may have a love for adventure, exploration, swimming, snorkeling, or S.C.U.B.A. diving, or water, or may like to
remain unseen, hidden below the surface. Nightbane with this
Morphus have the Swimming skill at 80%, can breathe underwater for 2D6+20 minutes at a time whether air tanks are present
or not, and have a depth tolerance of 1,000 feet (305 m) unless
stated otherwise.
