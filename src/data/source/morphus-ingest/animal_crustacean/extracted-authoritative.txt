and playful creatures, and seem to possess almost human-like

when blinded, and are able to operate perfectly under such conditions. Unless otherwise mentioned, they also have a natural
Swimming skill of 90%, swimming speed is running Spd plus
50% (e.g. a character whose Spd on land is 24 can swim at a
speed of 36), but land Spd may be slower or nonexistent.
