Bear Form Table ------
Bears are respected and feared for their strength and raw 
power, but they are also seen as relatively benign creatures that 
rarely kill even for food, let alone sport. Slow to rage, a pro­
voked bear is nonetheless a lethal creature. Many Nightbanes 
who have ursine characteristics are big, gentle people, or strong 
at heart. The most common bear shapes include the grizzly, po­
lar, black and brown bears. Native American Nightbanes seem 
to subconsciously favor this species.
