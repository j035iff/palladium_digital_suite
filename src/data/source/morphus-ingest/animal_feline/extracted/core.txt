Feline Table -------
The player gets to select the features of a cat. Any feline, 
from prehistoric sabertooth tigers to household tabbies can be 
selected. Independent, lazy or sensual Nightbane tend to have 
some feline elements in their Morphus. Feline Nightbane may 
have triggered the persecution and killing of cats during the 
Middle Ages (especially during plague years) when cats were 
believed to be demonic creatures who had brought plague and 
disease to the world.
